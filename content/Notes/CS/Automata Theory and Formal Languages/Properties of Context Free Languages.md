## Normal Forms of CFG
### Chomskey Normal Form(CNF)
Have productions only in the following forms:
1. $A \to BC (A,B,C \in V)$
2. $A \to a (A \in V, a \in T)$
### Greibach Normal Form(GNF)
Have productions only in the following forms:
1. $A \to a\alpha$ ($A \in V, a \in T, \alpha \in V^*$)
